### Why Crypto Became Relevant

Two things really pushed crypto into the spotlight: the pandemic and the meme era.

### Beyond the Hype

Of course, not every crypto project is serious. Some are scams, some rise and fall overnight, and the market as a whole can feel more like a rollercoaster than a bank. But behind the noise, there are deeper shifts happening.

Crypto lets people send money across borders without the delays or fees of traditional banks. It allows communities to create their own forms of value, like tokens for artists, gamers, or local groups. It opens up the possibility of financial systems that are not controlled by a few central authorities.
